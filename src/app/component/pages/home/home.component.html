Welcome to Talent Checks!
Discover the only automated reference checking platform for both candidates and organizations. Candidates can effortlessly collect, securely store, and easily share video & text references with future employers. Organizations benefit from streamlined access to these reliable references, enhancing their hiring process. Experience efficiency and convenience at the click of a button with Talent Checks!
Why Talent Checks
Gather references once, use them effectively every time – saving time and effort for candidates and companies alike.
Navigating the job market can be demanding for both candidates and organizations. Candidates face the hassle of repeatedly soliciting references, while organizations grapple with the cumbersome process of verifying them. Talent Checks simplifies this for all. By automating the collection, storage, and sharing of references, we empower candidates and organizations to streamline their recruitment and hiring processes, ensuring a smoother journey for everyone involved.

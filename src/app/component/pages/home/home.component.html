Welcome to Talent Checks!
Talent Checks is the only automated reference checking technology in the market that allows candidates to collect video & text references, have them securely stored, and easily shared with potential future employers during the hiring process with a click of a button.
Why Talent Checks
Now you don’t have to worry about asking your network to submit multiple references every time you are applying to a job, because one time is now enough
Being active in the job market is taxing in itself, and we understand that going to your network several times to ask them to submit multiple reference checks to the companies you are applying for makes it even more challenging. Talent Checks is here to make this journey easier through automating the collection, storage, and sharing of your references while putting you at the driver seat!
